Development

You want to learn how to develop your own tasks and modules? You've come to the right place. We'll guide you through the entire process, one step after another. First, have a look at the Basics to see what skills are required and to learn more about the technologies used by Tatool. Next, you'll have to get familiar with the terms and concepts Tatool uses. Take a few minutes to read through the Glossary. Now it get's serious - get started and setup your local tatool environment. After that, you can go on and write your first own Executable.

You're fine with using the pre-existing tasks in Tatool's Task Library? Then you don't have to setup or develop anything, simply use the built-in tatool Module Editor. Take a look at the Create Modules section to learn more.
