open-source experiment software for researchers


Developing computer-based experiments just got a whole lot easier with tatool.

Flexible Standard

We use the well-known web technology standards HTML5, CSS, JavaScript and node.js. This makes it easy to work with tatool and even simpler to find a developer.

Built for researchers

We focused on the functionality that you need to get your research done. Go from programming, configuring experiments to collecting data and analysing it in no time.

Reach everyone, everywhere

Thanks to our web technologies you can include participants from all around the world by telling them to simply start up their favourite web browser.


Tatool Features

Experiment View

The actual experiment view comes with a set of pre-built features like feedback, trial, timer and level indicators. For stimuli presentation we provide easy to use html elements to display stimuli at specific positions on the screen.

Easy Publishing

As a developer you can decide to publish your experiment into the tatool repository as a public or a private module. Public modules can be installed by anyone and private modules can only be installed by the list of people you invite.



Experiment Editor

The Experiment Editor allows you to change an existing experiment quickly and easily without writing a single line of code. This even allows people with no programming knowledge to take existing experiments and customize them to their needs with own stimuli, timing, sequences, etc.

Data Export

As a Researcher your ultimate goal is to get access to the data collected during your experiment. Tatool provides a research view that gives you a detailed log of who's participating in your experiment and how many sessions they've completed. You can also conveniently download their data as a CSV file.
