Welcome

In the following task, you have to decide as quickly and as accurately as possible whether the print color of the word presented is green or blue.

Press A on your keyboard if the print color is green; press L on your keyboard if the print color is blue.

If a green check mark appears in the upper right corner, your decision was correct, otherwise a red cross will be displayed. Press the Escape key (ESC) on your keyboard to exit the training task at any time.

Start with the right arrow on your keyboard.
